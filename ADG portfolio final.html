Hello! My name is Adrian Davila and I am an artist who prioritizes in drawing characters which I have done for a very long time and is just something I am pretty used to by now. My approach to digital media is originally just from signing up for the class not knowing what to expect but soon after understanding what and how to figure things out for the assignments given to me in the class with new information such as understanding how to work with adobe products like illustrator and photoshop. I learned how to animate gifs, create art through code, make small form website and more. I believe it took me much more time than others to understand what went on during the assignments but I would say by now I have come to understand just how to deal with what has been given to me and have become a lot less stressful.

Pixelated

Bad Photoshop

Perfect Mask

We dealt with perfectly covering an image and also having a separate image be surrounded in a specific color theme.

Animated gif

We learned how to make a gif and a basic animating picture from side to side from a picture we chose.

Self portrait and Landscape

Tokens
